Healthy Salmon Recipe

Bellow is healthy and delicious recipe for your salmon that you're going to love.
It is really simple to make and is packed full of flavour and goodness. their are only
9 ingredients and will take you less than 15 minutes to make. I have also added 2 side
dishes for you to try if your not sure what to pair with your salmon. Be sure not to get
mixed up with the Tablespoon (tbsp) and Teaspoon (tsp).

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Organic Salmon Darnes
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1/3rd cup of honey
  • 1 tbsp Sirhacha sauce (adjust to your spice level)
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp Apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp of fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Start by pre-heating the oven at 200 degrees C
  2. Next were going to start by heating a pan and adding in your Butter, Honey and
    soy sauce until combined
  3. When that is done go ahead and start add the rest of the ingedients and combine
  4. Next you want to season your salmon with some salt
  5. Pop them into some oven bags,if you dont have oven bags some tin foil works fine
  6. Add your delicious sauce to the oven bags/foil and seal
  7. Lay them in an oven tray and put them in the oven for 12 mins
